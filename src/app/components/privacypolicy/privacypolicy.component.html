3.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our services, we collect:

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b)): To provide vehicle service tracking and management services

Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)): To improve our services, ensure security, and prevent fraud

Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Your data is NEVER:

  • ❌ Shared with advertisers or marketing companies

7. Data Storage and Security

7.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Secure Storage:
    • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
    • Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
    • Mobile devices use hardware-encrypted storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android)
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with 12-hour expiration
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and regular security audits

7.2 Data Location

Your data is stored on secure servers located within the European Union. If data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Other legally approved transfer mechanisms

7.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Service History: Retained for 7 years after last service (legal requirement for vehicle records)
  • Log Data: Retained for 90 days
  • Deleted Account Data: Permanently deleted within 10 days of account deletion request

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

8.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

8.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)

Request deletion of your personal data when:

  • Data is no longer necessary for the purposes collected
  • You withdraw consent
  • You object to processing
  • Data was unlawfully processed

Exception: We may retain data required by law (e.g., vehicle service records).

8.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)

Request limitation of how we use your data in certain circumstances.

8.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV).

8.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7(3))

Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., "Remember Me" feature).

8.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

File a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

For Sweden: Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY)

Website: https://www.imy.se

Email: imy@imy.se
