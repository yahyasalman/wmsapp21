Getting Started

How It Works

When you bring your vehicle to an authorized workshop, they register it in the Digital Workshop Service system. Once registered, your vehicle will appear in the app automatically when you sign in.

1. Creating Your Account

  1. Download the Digital Workshop Service app from the App Store or Google Play Store
  2. Open the app and tap "Sign Up"
  3. Enter the email address you provided to the workshop
  4. Create a strong password
  5. Enter your phone number
  6. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  7. Your account is now active and your registered vehicles will appear!
Important: Use the same email address that you gave to the workshop. Your vehicles are linked to this email in our system.

2. Viewing Your Vehicles

Once you've created your account with the same email registered at the workshop, your vehicles will automatically appear in the app. You can then view their complete service history.

Account Management

Changing Your Password

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Change Password"
  4. Enter your current password
  5. Enter and confirm your new password
  6. Tap "Update Password"

Updating Your Profile

  1. Go to Settings → Account Settings
  2. Tap "Edit Profile"
  3. Update your name, email, or phone number as needed
  4. Tap "Save Changes"

Language Preferences

You can change the app language in Settings → Language. Currently supported languages:

  • English
  • Swedish (Svenska)

Deleting Your Account

If you want to delete your account, please contact our support team:

Email: support@digitalworkshop.nu

In your email, include your email address and request account deletion. We will process your request and permanently delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

Your Vehicles

Viewing Your Vehicles

Once you've created your account with the email address registered at the workshop, all your vehicles will automatically appear in the app. They are managed by the workshop in the system.

Vehicle Information

For each vehicle, you can view:

  • Vehicle make, model, and year
  • Registration number
  • VIN (Vehicle Identification Number)
  • Complete service and maintenance history

If Your Vehicle Doesn't Appear

  1. Make sure you're using the same email address you provided to the workshop
  2. Sign out and sign back in
  3. Refresh the app or close and reopen it
  4. Contact the workshop to confirm your vehicle has been registered

Service History

Viewing Your Service History

  1. Select a vehicle from your vehicle list
  2. Tap "Service History"
  3. You'll see all recorded services, maintenance, and repairs
  4. Tap on any service record to view details

Understanding Service Records

Each service record includes:

  • Date: When the service was performed
  • Workshop: Which authorized workshop performed the service
  • Service Type: Regular service, repair, maintenance, etc.
  • Mileage: Vehicle mileage at time of service
  • Details: Specific work performed
  • Invoice: Digital copy of the invoice (if available)

Downloading Service Records

  1. Open a service record
  2. Tap the download icon
  3. Select your preferred format (PDF)
  4. The document will be saved to your device

Sharing Service Records

  1. Open a service record
  2. Tap the share icon
  3. Choose your sharing method (email, message, etc.)
  4. Complete the sharing process
Benefit: Digital service records help with resale value documentation and warranty claims.

Workshop Support

Registering Your Vehicle at a Workshop

To have your vehicle appear in the app:

  1. Bring your vehicle to an authorized workshop
  2. Provide your email address to the workshop
  3. The workshop will register your vehicle in the system
  4. Sign up in the app with that same email address
  5. Your vehicle and service history will appear automatically

Important: Use the Same Email

Make sure you provide the same email address to the workshop that you use when creating your app account. This links your vehicles to your account.

Service Records

After each service at the workshop, your service records will automatically appear in the app. You don't need to do anything - the workshop handles this for you.

Troubleshooting

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. You'll receive a link to create a new password. The link is valid for 24 hours.

The app is not loading my vehicle information. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection (WiFi or mobile data)
  2. Close the app completely and reopen it
  3. Make sure you're using the latest version of the app
  4. Try clearing the app cache (Settings → Apps → Digital Workshop → Storage → Clear Cache)
  5. If the problem persists, contact support
I entered the wrong registration number/VIN. Can I change it?

Vehicle information is managed by the workshop and cannot be changed by you through the app. If there's an error, please contact the workshop where your vehicle is registered and ask them to correct the information.

The app keeps logging me out. Why?

This could happen for security reasons:

  • Your session may have expired after a period of inactivity
  • You may have changed your password from another device
  • The app may need to be updated

Try:

  1. Update the app to the latest version
  2. Clear the app cache
  3. Restart your device
  4. Contact support if the issue continues
My service history is not showing. What's wrong?

Service history depends on workshop records. If your history is empty:

  • Your vehicle may not have any recorded services yet
  • The workshop may not have access to record services
  • You may need to grant workshop permissions

Contact your workshop to ensure they have your vehicle information and proper access permissions.

The app is crashing or freezing. How do I fix this?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Force close the app completely
  2. Restart your device
  3. Update the app to the latest version from App Store/Play Store
  4. Clear the app cache (Settings → Apps → Digital Workshop → Storage → Clear Cache)
  5. Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
  6. If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the app

If crashes continue, please contact support with the error details.

I'm having trouble with the "Remember Me" feature. How do I manage it?

The "Remember Me" feature securely stores your login credentials:

  • Check "Remember Me" on login to stay signed in longer
  • Uncheck it if you're on a shared device
  • To disable it, go to Settings → Privacy & Data → Clear Saved Credentials

Your data is encrypted using your device's secure storage.

How do I update the app?

For iOS:

  1. Open the App Store
  2. Go to the "Updates" tab
  3. Find "Digital Workshop Service" and tap "Update"

For Android:

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Search for "Digital Workshop Service"
  3. If an update is available, tap "Update"

Keeping your app updated ensures you get the latest features and security improvements.

I see an error message. What does it mean?

Common error messages and solutions:

  • "Network Error": Check your internet connection
  • "Authentication Failed": Verify your login credentials; try resetting your password
  • "Vehicle Not Found": Check that your registration number or VIN is correct
  • "Server Unavailable": Please try again later; our servers may be under maintenance

If you see an error code, please share it with support for faster assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure in the app?

Yes! We use industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transfers
  • Secure storage on encrypted servers
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • GDPR compliant data handling

See our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Can I use the app offline?

The app requires an internet connection for most features. However, previously loaded data (like recent service history) may be cached locally. For the best experience, please ensure you have an active internet connection.

How many vehicles can I add to my account?

You don't manually add vehicles. All vehicles registered at workshops with your email address will automatically appear in your app account.

Who can see my vehicle information?

Only you can see your vehicle information and service history in the app. Your data is private and secure. The authorized workshop has access to record your service information, but they manage this from their own system.

What should I do if I suspect a security breach?

If you believe your account has been compromised:

  1. Change your password immediately
  2. Review your account activity in Settings
  3. Revoke access from any unrecognized workshops
  4. Contact support immediately at support@digitalworkshop.nu
How often is the app updated?

We regularly release updates with new features, improvements, and security patches. Major updates are released quarterly, with critical security updates as needed. Always keep your app updated to the latest version.

What are the system requirements?

Android: Android 7.0 or later

Internet: WiFi or mobile data connection required
